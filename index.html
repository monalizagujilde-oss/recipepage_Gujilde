Tap-Si-Log

Price: 80

Category: Filipino Breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup tapa (cured beef), sliced into thin strips

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
